        No 28. During its chart run Deconstruction PB 43055 was withdrawn and Deconstruction PB 43241 was released. Sales of both were combined to produce a chart position.

        No 35. The label of Rhythm King LEFT 34 shows the title of the a-side is 'Hey DJ/I Can't Dance To That Music You're Playing'. It is a medley of two songs. The aa-side is 'Ska Train'.

        No 42. The label of RCA PB 43037 shows the title is 'Mental'.

        No 73. The label of EMI EM 95 shows the title is '(What's Wrong With) Dreaming?'.

        No 74. The label of China CHINA 18 shows the title is 'Yebo'. The artist is 'The Art Of Noise featuring Mahlathini And The Mahotella Queens'.
