        So here is my usual review of the UK charts today:
        - Well it looks like its been a big week for both dance music & the 'Suicide Squad' soundtrack. Firstly, congratulations to: Major Lazer, Justin Bieber & MO for a 3rd consecutive week at #1 on the singles chart with 'Cold Water'. It was great seeing another JB vs JB battle on the charts today & this also happened with Bieber's other big hits, 'Sorry' & 'Love Yourself'. Here's another interesting point... if JB is #1 again next week, he'll have spent his 17th week at the top this decade, which then becomes more than any other act has done in the 2010s. Great for DJ Snake as well as 'Let Me Love You' is this week's highest new entry at #2.
        - As I mentioned earlier, the 'Suicide Squad' film release last Friday has helped boost some of the songs from it, with the biggest being 'Heathens' & gives 21 Pilots their first Top 10 appearance in the UK as a result. The biggest climber across the Top 40 came from The Chainsmokers & Halsey, as 'Closer' leaped from 33 to 12, up 21 places. The Chainsmokers' look very likely to earn their 2nd Top 10 hit next Friday & this is my favourite song from the duo so far. 'Sunshine' was also new to the Top 40 today, which is nice to see considering it's summertime! :)
        - Regarding next week, 'You Don't Know Love' by Olly Murs will take a big jump back up the Top 40 thanks to the discount on iTunes fuelling its sales, but I can't see any major new entries that will go anywhere near the top for the next chart. Over on the albums chart tonight, Blossoms' self-titled debut album is new at #1... so very good job to them.
