        This was the week I left the UK after a year at an MSc at University of Birmingham, I remember listening to Mercia Sound, BRMB, Northamps 96, and Radio 1 to Spagna, Bananarama, MJackson`s "Bad", Rick Astley and Cliff Richard. The Bee Gees`s "You win again" made a strong impression on me, and I vividly recall seeing their new album on display at a record shop on Bristol Road on that last week, just off the then southern University`s entrance. The brothers` comeback from a hiatus was news at that moment, being that they had dominated the airwaves in Mexico at the end of the seventies.Thanks for keeping these lists on line.
